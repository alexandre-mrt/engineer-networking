# Research: AI Impact on Software Engineering Jobs (2024-2026)

## Code Generation & Adoption
- 46% of code written by GitHub Copilot users is AI-generated (GitHub Octoverse 2025)
- GitHub Copilot: 4.7M paid subscribers (Jan 2026), 20M total users, 90% of Fortune 100
- AI coding tools market: $7.37B in 2025, Copilot holds 42% share
- 84% of developers use or plan to use AI tools; 51% use them daily
- 80% of new GitHub developers use Copilot within first week
- Gartner: 90% of enterprise engineers will use AI assistants by 2028 (up from <14% in 2024)

## Productivity Gains
- 55.8% faster task completion with Copilot (study of 4,800 devs)
- 30-60% time savings on coding, test generation, documentation
- Success rates improved from 70% to 78% with Copilot
- McKinsey: high performers saw 16-30% improvements in productivity, quality

## Roles Most at Risk
- **Computer programmers**: 74.5% observed AI task exposure (Anthropic 2026)
- **Junior developers**: 54% of engineering leaders plan to hire fewer juniors
- Entry-level postings fell 29-35% globally since Jan 2024
- Employment for developers aged 22-25 declined ~20% from 2022 peak
- 70% of hiring managers believe AI can do intern jobs
- **QA/Testing**: manual testing declining, automated test design still valuable
- **DevOps**: relatively safe — AI can't replicate cloud architecture design

## Job Market Data
- 600+ rounds of tech layoffs in 2025, ~211K employees affected
- Programmer employment fell 27.5% (2023-2025), but software developers only -0.3%
- Entry-level hiring at 15 biggest tech firms fell 25% (2023-2024)
- Jobs requiring ≤3 years experience: dropped from 43% to 28%
- AI/ML job postings up 85% YoY; generative AI postings up 170%
- 540,000 new engineering jobs projected (2025, Worldmetrics)

## Expert Quotes
- **Jensen Huang (NVIDIA)**: "You're not going to lose your job to an AI, but you're going to lose your job to someone who uses AI"
- **Sam Altman (OpenAI)**: "AI can already perform the tasks of junior-level employees"
- **Boris Cherny (Anthropic)**: Software engineer title could be gone by end of 2026, replaced by "builder"
- **Satya Nadella (Microsoft)**: Warned AI risks becoming economic bubble if benefits stay concentrated

## Counter-Arguments
- AI handles routine 70% of code, but critical 30% (architecture, security, business logic) remains human
- Essential human attributes: creativity, communication, ethics, empathy, quality control
- Employment expected to grow 17.9% (2023-2033)
- Software engineer listings on Indeed up 11% annually
- "AI won't replace developers, it will make the best ones indispensable"
